Section 5 ‚Ä¢ 5 minutes
The Twist

‚ö†Ô∏è This is the key moment

Build it up. Make them feel the pain of non-reproducibility.

üí¨
Say:
"Beautiful. You just did real empirical research. Question, data, analysis, visualization.

Now I need everyone to do one thing: Close Stata completely. Not minimize‚Äîclose it."
‚è≥
Wait for everyone to close Stata
üí¨
Then say:
"Okay. Open Stata again.

You have 2 minutes to reproduce that graph. The one showing education and income with the fitted line. Go."
üìù
Watch the chaos

Don't help. Let them struggle. After 2 minutes:

"Stop. Raise your hand if you successfully reproduced the graph."

(Probably 0-2 hands)

"This is the most important lesson today. If you can't reproduce it, you didn't do it.

Now imagine it's 3 weeks from now. You're revising your paper. Your advisor asks 'what if we control for population?' You have no idea what you did.

After break, we're going to fix this."

Section 6 ‚Ä¢ 20 minutes
The Solution
üí¨
Say:
"Everything we just did‚Äîwe're going to redo it. But this time, in a way we can reproduce forever."
‚å®Ô∏è
Create a do-file together (File ‚Üí New ‚Üí Do-file):

/*============================================================================== Project: Education and Income Analysis Author: [Your Name] Date: February 18, 2026 Purpose: Examine relationship between college graduation rates and income ==============================================================================*/ * SETUP clear all set more off cd "/path/to/your/project" * LOAD DATA import delimited "state_economics.csv", clear describe summarize * ANALYSIS eststo clear eststo m1: regress median_income college_grad_rate eststo m2: regress median_income college_grad_rate cost_of_living_index esttab m1 m2, se r2 label * FIGURE twoway (scatter median_income college_grad_rate, mcolor(navy%50)) /// (lfit median_income college_grad_rate, lcolor(cranberry)), /// title("College Graduation and Median Income") graph export "education_income.png", replace
üí¨
After running it (Ctrl+D / Cmd+D):
"There. In 30 seconds, we reproduced everything.

And if your advisor asks for a change next month? You open this file, make the edit, re-run. Done."
